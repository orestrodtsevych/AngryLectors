Angry Lectors - mobile video game of the runner-platform genre for Android. Developed by students of Lviv National University name of Ivan Franko The main characters are a student and a teacher. The average statistical student postpones all deadlines for the last days or for the last hours. A good teacher wants the student to pass everything as soon as possible, so the chase begins in the corridors of the university.


The main goal of the game is that the player-controlled character ran as far as possible, avoiding oncoming obstacles. Since we are real students, we want to put it off for later. If a character encounters something, the inspector catches up with him and grabs (regardless of how far he managed to escape) and the game ends with the student having to close all deadlines.
